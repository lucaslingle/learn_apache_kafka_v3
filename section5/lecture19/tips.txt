If the landing page for multiplatform conduktor via docker compose isn't loading, 
you may instead see a login page that does not offer account creation. 

In that case, to set the admin credentials, you should go to conduktor-kafka-single.yml
and edit the appropriate subsection of that docker-compose config to include

services:
  conduktor-console:
    environment:
      CDK_ADMIN_EMAIL: "admin@conduktor.io"
      CDK_ADMIN_PASSWORD: YOUR_CHOICE_OF_PASSWORD

Entering that email and password will allow you to circumvent the lack of a landing page
for account creation.
